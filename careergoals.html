My Career Goals

I aim to grow as a back-end developer, mastering server-side technologies, database design, and API development. Over the next few years, I plan to deepen my expertise in Node.js, Express.js, and RESTful API architecture, while gaining hands-on experience with cloud platforms such as AWS and Azure.

I also hope to contribute to large-scale projects that require scalable, secure, and high-performance solutions. Learning best practices in database optimization, unit testing, and CI/CD pipelines is a key part of my professional growth.

My long-term goal is to become a senior back-end engineer who can lead teams, design robust architectures, and mentor junior developers, while continuously exploring emerging technologies in cloud computing, microservices, and DevOps practices.

I am also interested in working collaboratively with front-end teams to build seamless integrations, ensuring that users have a smooth and efficient experience with every application I help develop.
